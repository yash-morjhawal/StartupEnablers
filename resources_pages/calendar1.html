August 2025 Compliance Calendar for Startups, Businesses and Individuals

Here are the key deadlines for August 2025 to help you stay compliant and avoid penalties.

Early August

  • 7th August: TDS/TCS Deposit.
  • 10th August: GST Returns (GSTR-7 & GSTR-8).
  • 11th August: GSTR-1 Filing (Monthly).
  • 13th August: GSTR-1 IFF, GSTR-5, GSTR-6 Filing.

Mid-August

  • 15th August: Issuance of TDS Certificates (Form 16A & 27D).
  • 20th August: GSTR-3B & GSTR-5A Filing.

End of August

  • 30th August: Furnishing Challan-cum-Statement for specific TDS sections (26QB, 26QC, 26QD, 26QE).
