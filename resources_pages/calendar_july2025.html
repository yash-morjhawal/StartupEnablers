July 2025 Compliance Calendar for Startups, Businesses and Individuals

Here’s your guide to the important compliance deadlines for July 2025.

  • 7th July: TDS/TCS Deposit for June 2025.
  • 10th July: GST Returns (GSTR-7 & GSTR-8) for June 2025.
  • 11th July: GSTR-1 Filing (Monthly).
  • 13th July: GSTR-1 IFF, GSTR-5, GSTR-6 Filing.
  • 15th July: Issuance of TDS Certificates (Form 16A & 27D) and PF & ESI Payments/Returns.
  • 15th July: Annual Return on Foreign Liabilities and Assets (FLA).
  • 18th July: CMP-08 Filing for April–June 2025.
  • 20th July: GSTR-3B & GSTR-5A Filing.
  • 22nd July: GSTR-3B Filing (Quarterly) for April–June 2025.
  • 30th July: Furnishing Challan-cum-Statement for TDS under sections 194-IA, 194-IB, 194M, 194S.
  • 31st July: TDS Return Filing (Form 24Q, 26Q, 27Q, 27EQ) for Q1 FY 2025-26.
