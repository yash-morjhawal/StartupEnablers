IFSCA Approves “Platform Play” for Fund Management Entities at GIFT IFSC

Published: June 24, 2025

In a significant stride towards enhancing the appeal and accessibility of India’s International Financial Services Centre (IFSC) at GIFT City, the International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA) has approved a groundbreaking “Platform Play” model for Fund Management Entities (FMEs)...

Key Conditions Under the New Framework

  • Additional net worth requirement: USD 500,000 above existing thresholds.
  • Mandatory Principal Officer for each scheme.
  • Transition to dedicated FME model when corpus exceeds USD 50 million.

This progressive move is expected to attract a wider array of fund managers and schemes, fostering growth and diversification within the IFSC.
