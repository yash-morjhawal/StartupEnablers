SEBI Mandates New Certification Norms for AIF Managers

Published: June 25, 2025

The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has officially unveiled revised certification requirements for key investment personnel of Alternative Investment Fund (AIF) managers...

Category-Wise Certification Now Mandatory

  • Category I & II AIFs: Pass NISM Series-XIX-C or XIX-D exams.
  • Category III AIFs: Pass NISM Series-XIX-C or XIX-E exams.

Deadline and Industry Impact

All existing AIFs must comply by July 31, 2025... The emphasis on standardized knowledge is expected to foster greater investor confidence and promote best practices across the alternative investment sector in India.
