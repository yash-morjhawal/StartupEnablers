Union Budget 2025 – Startups, Investors & GIFT IFSC

Published: 2025

The Union Budget 2025 focuses on fiscal prudence, infrastructure-led growth, and fostering the startup ecosystem with ₹10,000 crore Funds of Funds and deep-tech support...

Key Growth Drivers

  1. Taxation reforms
  2. Power sector boost
  3. Urban infrastructure expansion
  4. Mining policy updates
  5. Financial stability measures
  6. Regulatory ease of doing business

Startups & MSMEs

  • Tax holiday till March 2030.
  • Deep Tech Fund support.
  • Revised MSME classification.

GIFT IFSC Focus

Extended tax exemptions till 2030, simplified compliance, and exemptions for offshore derivative investments.

Conclusion

Budget 2025 builds a foundation for a globally competitive economy while incentivizing innovation and investment.
