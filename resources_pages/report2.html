The Maha Economy of Mahakumbh 2025: A Religious and Economic Powerhouse

Published: 2025

Mahakumbh 2025 was more than a spiritual gathering — it became a massive economic catalyst with 660 million attendees from 76 countries generating ₹3 lakh crore (~$36B) in transactions...

Scale & Tourism

  • 660M visitors vs Haj (2.5M), Rio Carnival (7M), Oktoberfest (7.2M).
  • Projected religious tourism revenue: $59B by 2028.

Economic Impact

Infrastructure upgrades, job creation (60 lakh), ₹54,000 crore in state revenue.

Commerce & Spending

  • Pooja essentials: ₹2,000 crore
  • Hospitality: ₹2,500 crore
  • Groceries: ₹11,500 crore

Innovation & Startups

From fintech QR payments to AI parking, startups like Vama, Swiggy Instamart, and Paytm innovated on-site.

Conclusion

Mahakumbh 2025 proved faith and commerce can co-exist, driving massive economic and cultural gains.
