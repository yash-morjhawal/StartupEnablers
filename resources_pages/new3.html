CBDT Notifies TDS Exemption for Payments to IFSC Units (Effective July 1, 2025)

Published: June 20, 2025

The Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) has issued Notification No. 67/2025, effective July 1, 2025, exempting certain payments to eligible units in GIFT City IFSC from TDS...

What the IFSC Unit (Payee) Must Do

  • Submit Form 1 annually.
  • Verify Form 1 each year for the tax holiday period.
  • Ensure income is from approved activities.

What the Payer Must Do

  • Stop TDS only after receiving Form 1.
  • Report exempt payments in quarterly TDS returns.
  • Retain Form 1 for audits.

This move reinforces the government’s commitment to developing GIFT City as a globally competitive financial hub.
