The Debt Market at IFSC: Key Insights & Trends (2024–2025)

Published: 2025

The International Financial Services Centre (IFSC) at GIFT City has emerged as a pivotal platform for Indian financial institutions to tap into international capital markets...

Market Size and Composition

  • Cumulative Issuance: USD 6.99 billion across 57 listings in FY 2024–25.
  • Sectoral Distribution: NBFCs dominated with USD 5.23 billion from 50 issuances.
  • Issuer Profile: Top issuers include Muthoot Finance, Continuum Trinethra, SBI, REC, Shriram Finance.

Instrument Analysis

95% fixed-rate bonds (USD 6.66B) vs 5% floating-rate bonds (USD 329.2M). Fixed rates averaged 6.63%; floating linked to SOFR averaged +4.43%.

Sustainable Finance

ESG-focused bonds formed 39.4% of issuance, led by green bonds worth USD 1.455B.

Market Infrastructure

Trustee split: Foreign trustees (USD 5.415B) and Indian trustees (USD 1.15B).

Credit Ratings

Rated issuances: 45.6% (USD 4.63B high-yield, USD 1.63B investment-grade).

Key Takeaways

  • USD 6.99B debt raised in FY 2024–25.
  • NBFC dominance.
  • Strong ESG adoption.
  • Preference for fixed-rate bonds.
