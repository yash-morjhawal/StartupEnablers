June 2025 Compliance Calendar for Startups, Businesses and Individuals

Compliance management is critical for startups and businesses in India to avoid penalties and ensure smooth operations. Here are the key deadlines for June 2025.

  • 7th June: TDS/TCS Deposits for May 2025.
  • 10th June: Professional Tax Payments and Returns (state-specific).
  • Throughout June: GST filings (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-7, GSTR-8, GSTR-5, GSTR-6).
  • 15th June: Issuance of TDS Certificates (Forms 16, 16A, 16B, 16C, 16D).
  • 15th June: First Instalment of Advance Tax for FY 2025-26.
  • 29th & 30th June: Annual Filings for Nidhi Companies and Deposit Returns.
  • 30th June: Professional Tax Remittances (state-specific).
