Important Notes for Applicants

Note Description
Guidelines Each grant listed above comes with its own set of eligibility criteria, funding scope, and application timeline. Founders are advised to carefully read official program guidelines before applying. Submitting a well-prepared proposal can significantly improve your chances of selection.
Usage Grants can be used for various purposes â€” from product development and testing to marketing and global expansion. While most grants do not require equity dilution, they often demand proof of concept, innovation, and market potential.

Impact By securing a government or institutional grant, you not only gain financial backing but also credibility in the eyes of investors, customers, and partners. It can act as a powerful catalyst in scaling your venture to the next level.
